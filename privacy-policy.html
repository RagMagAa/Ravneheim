Privacy Policy

Our commitment to your privacy and data security

Last Updated: April 7, 2025

Introduction

Ravneheim ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our desktop applications, including the IMU (Image Management Utility) application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our applications.

Zero Data Collection Policy

Ravneheim applications are completely self-contained desktop applications that operate entirely on your local device. We want to make it explicitly clear that:

  • We do not collect any personal information from you
  • We do not track your usage of our applications
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or any other tracking mechanisms
  • Our applications do not communicate with our servers
  • We do not have access to any data you process using our applications

All data processing occurs locally on your device, and we have no capability to access, collect, or transmit this data.

Cloud Storage Integration

Some of our applications, such as IMU, offer optional integration with third-party cloud storage services like Google Drive. When you choose to connect our application with these services:

  • You will be directed to the service provider's authentication page (e.g., Google) to grant permission
  • We have no access to any of those files in any way
  • Access tokens may be stored locally on your device to maintain your connection
  • We have no access to account passwords
  • Communication occurs directly between our application on your device and the third-party service
  • We do not have any servers that process or store your cloud storage data

This integration is completely optional and only functions if you choose to enable it.

Third-Party Services

Our applications may integrate with third-party services (such as Google Drive) at your explicit request. Please note:

  • Your use of these third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services before use
  • We have no control over the content or policies of these third-party services

For Google Drive integration specifically, please review Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google processes your data.

Application Purchases

Our applications are distributed through third-party platforms (such as Payhip). Please note:

  • Any information you provide during purchase is collected and processed by the distribution platform, not by us
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • We may receive minimal purchase verification information from distribution platforms to validate licenses

For information on how your data is handled during the purchase process, please refer to the privacy policy of the respective distribution platform.

Your Data Rights

Since we do not collect or process your personal data, traditional data rights requests (such as access, correction, or deletion) are not applicable to our relationship with you.

For any data stored locally by our applications on your device (such as application settings or cloud storage access tokens), you maintain complete control and can manage this data through the application interface or by accessing the application's storage location on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

  • We will never refactor into existing purchased applications any data tracking or collection. If any of our future applications will have any of these, it will be clearly stated and this policy will be updated.

Contact Us
